2018 AHAA Australian National Championships

The Queensland Branch will be hosting the 2018 AHAA Australian National Championships.  They will be held on 4th, 5th & 6th at the Queensland State Equestrian Centre (QSEC).

There will be competitor goodie bags, garlands, trophies and an incredible line up of sponsors!  This is our NATIONAL Baroque Show - for Andalusians, Iberians, Friesians, Lusitanos, Lipizzaners, Part-Breds.  There are travel incentive packages for AHAA meembers so that distance isn't so much of an issue!  Support your breed by supporting our Nationals!

Ridden classes on the Sunday
Dressage (at QSEC outdoor sand arenas)
