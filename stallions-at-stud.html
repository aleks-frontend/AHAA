Jaleador DE JC III x Alondra XXIX 
2010 - 16hh - Grey - PRE Spanish Stallion

- Approved breeding stallion with ANCCE Spanish Studbook
- Ridden & Handled by FEI Grade Ib Para Equestrian
- Imported from NZ at 6 months of ag
- Scored up to 69.78% in Official Novice out of a field of 15 horses
- Scoring 8s for canter movements, lengthen trots and paces
** National AHAA Champion Rider over 18yrs 2015
** National AHAA Reserve Champion Ridden Hack 2015
** State Champion Led Junior Stallion 2014
** State Reserve Champion Ridden Hack 2014

HANNA PODEROSO is a beautiful, tall, strong boned, correct stallion that holds very impressive dressage genetics; sharing some of his genetics with PRE stallion Olympic sensation FUEGO DE CARDENAS via his sire line.  HANNA PODEROSO also shares genetics with UBIO, the first ever PRE stallion licensed for a German Warmblood Studbook, and ILLES, who is one the dressage mounts of Victor Alvarez. These genetics alone make HANNA PODEROSO a valuable breeding stallion for future dressage mounts.
At such a young age HANNA PODEROSO has wowed the public and judges with his stunning Spanish looks, temperament and THAT impressive movement - freedom in the shoulders, forward, powerful hock action and a jaw dropping extension!
HANNA PODEROSO is owned, handled and ridden by Australian High Performance Para Equestrian; he has proven to be a sensible, reliable riding mount for Victoria who is aiming to campaign him in the qualification events for WEG 2018 and Paralympics 2020.

Picaro PM (Imp - Spain)

Not Just Another Horse ...2009 Perlino Stallion
EMH 16+hh
AHAA: SO1687   SSB: 724015090133479 PRE APTO   Dilutes Australia: WS00110
Sire: Revoltoso XXIX (Cremello, 16.3hh)     Dam: Doctora XXXVII (Buckskin, 16hh)
Bred by, and imported from, the world renowned Paco Marti stud, PICARO PM shows no compromise in type, conformation and temperament.  His generous bone complements his structure and he has an unrivalled style, elegance, beauty and presence that has captured the attention and inspired awe in those who have seen him firsthand.  Picaro’s temperament is typical of the Spanish horse – charismatic, affectionate, friendly and noble.
Movement is another of Picaro’s outstanding features with his four undeniably impressive paces – an active walk with an excellent length of stride; a naturally cadenced and elevated trot with exceptional extensions; a rhythmic, fluid and balanced canter and powerful gallop.  All of Picaro’s paces are executed with clarity and straightness with superb freedom of movement and expression as can be seen on his YouTube video.
To top it off, this extremely rare young PRE Apto stallion presents an exciting opportunity for astute breeders to introduce a cream gene to their colour pool – regardless of the mare’s colour.  Picaro PM is guaranteed to produce buckskin or palomino progeny to non-grey mares, with non-grey, dilute progeny also possible from grey mares depending upon her individual genetics. Picaro PM will not throw pink skin/blue eyes to his progeny unless the mare is already carrying a cream gene.  (Please ask for more information regarding the likely outcomes of a mating to your mare).
Recently described as ‘the best perlino who exists’ by a European breeder of PRE and ‘he ticks all the boxes of excellence for the breed standard’ by a qualified judge, Picaro PM continues to prove himself as a respected sire, passing on his unmistakeable qualities to his foals.  Impressive buckskin progeny by Picaro PM for sale.
