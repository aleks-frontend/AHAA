Welcome

Welcome to the website for the Andalusian Horse Association of Australasia which has been established since 1973.

This association holds the Stud Books in Australasia for the Purebred Spanish Horse, the Australian Andalusian, the Hispano-Arabe, the Partbred Andalusian, the Andalusian Derivative and shares, with the Lusitano Association of Australasia, the Stud Book for the Purebred Iberian Horse (Pure Spanish x Pure Lusitano).

The Association publishes a regular magazine about Andalusians in Australasia and also runs shows and seminars promoting this unique and ancient breed.

Please use the buttons on the left to find out more about Andalusians in Australia.

2018 AHAA Australian National Championships

The AHAA 2018 National Championships will be hosted by the Queensland Branch and held on 4th, 5th & 6th at the Queensland State Equestrian Centre (QSEC).  The program is on the Qld State Branch page.
