National Championships 2014

National Champion Ridden Andalusian - Valentina ridden by Chris Fowles
National Champion Spanish Entire - Mayville Lodge Nicholas owned by Susanne Brundell
National Champion Spanish Female - Anchorbar Alsana owned by Merle Ireland
Mayville Lodge Nicholas and Susanne Brundell - National Champion Ridden Spanish Andalusian
Harmony Hills Argon - National Champion Junior Spanish Entire
Harmony Hills Ernesto owned and ridden by Sheree Conroy - Senior High Point Andalusian of the Show
Omaru Coletta - National Champion Ridden Spanish Female. Rider, Perry Nancarrow, also won National Champion Youth Equitation.
Omaru Esperanza and Michelle Black - National Reserve Champion Ridden Spanish Female
GL Aurelio - National Junior Champion Purebred Iberian Entire
GL Conquistador - National Champion Lusitano Entire
Omaru Aleito and Dianne Baxter - National Reserve Champion Spanish Entire
Harmony Hills Ernesto and Sheree Conroy - Winner of Fiesta Costume Class
Omaru Coletta ridden by Perry Nancarro and Kristy Byrne - 3rd in Traditional Spanish Attire Class
Pas de Trois with Dianne Baxter on Omaru Aleito, Perry Nancarrow on Omaru Coletta and Michelle Black on Omaru Esperanza - winner of Exhibition Class
Tara Aztec Rose and Mia Pace – Youth Handler 10 years and under. Tara Aztec Rose, handled by Kaye Pace, won Senior Champion Partbred Female and Reserve Champion Ridden Partbred Female.

18 - 20 APRIL 2014 at TOOWOOMBA SHOWGROUNDS, QUEENSLAND
